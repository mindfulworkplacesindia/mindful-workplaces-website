The Core Team

Meet the Mindfullers.

Pankhuri Malhotra
Founder and Lead Facilitator
Pankhuri brings over a decade of experience across psychology, organisational behaviour, and workforce training in Australia and India. She has worked across government, research, and social sector organisations, giving her a ground-level understanding of how emotional load affects the people doing the hardest jobs. She is certified in Psychological First Aid and Trauma-Informed Practice, and leads Mindful Workplaces' facilitation, programme design, and client relationships. She served as President of Rotary Club District 3080 from 2013 to 2015 and was present at India's Polio-free declaration in 2014.
Psychological First Aid Trauma-Informed Practice 10+ Years Public Health India and Australia Rotary District 3080 President
Dr Pernasi Malhotra
Clinical Advisor and Co-Facilitator
A General Practitioner specialised in Public Health with professional experience in Australia, Dr Pernasi brings frontline clinical experience combined with public health systems thinking. As Clinical Advisor, she ensures every Mindful Workplaces programme is ethically grounded, clinically credible, and firmly non-therapeutic, appropriate for organisational settings. She co-facilitates in healthcare and high-responsibility environments.
MBBS MPH General Practitioner Public Health Clinical Advisory
Pallavi Sharma
Psychological Advisor
Pallavi holds a Masters in Clinical Psychology and a Postgraduate Diploma in Guidance and Counselling. She has experience across government hospitals, clinical and community settings, and workplace environments, and has facilitated workshops on mental health, wellbeing, and personal development. At Mindful Workplaces, she brings a practical, evidence-based understanding of human behaviour, combining psychological expertise with people-focused experience to support impactful programme delivery.
MA Clinical Psychology PG Diploma Guidance and Counselling Workshop Facilitation
